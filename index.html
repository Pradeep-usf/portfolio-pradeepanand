Projects

Customer Segmentation with Clustering

I explored three datasets (Synthetic, Mall Customers, and Wine Quality) and tried multiple clustering methods-K-Means, Hierarchical/Agglomerative, DBSCAN, and GMM. After standardizing features, I used the Elbow curve and Silhouette score to pick a good K for K-Means, and compared clusters using Silhouette, Davies-Bouldin, and Calinski-Harabasz. The segments were interpretable (e.g., value vs. high-spend groups), and DBSCAN helped when I suspected noise. Tech: pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib.

Fitness Center Retention (Churn) Analysis

I analyzed churn for a fitness center, then trained Logistic Regression, KNN, Random Forest, and SVM to predict who might leave. I did EDA, encoded/scaled features, and compared models on accuracy, precision, recall, and F1. Usage frequency and tenure were consistently important signals; tree models captured non-linear effects, while Logistic Regression gave a transparent baseline. Tech: pandas, scikit-learn, matplotlib.
